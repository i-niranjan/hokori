## What is Hokori?

Most creators fall into one of these categories:

- A simple link-in-bio that barely says anything about them.
- A massive personal portfolio website that takes weeks to build and maintain.

**Hokori sits somewhere in between.**

Hokori is a profile platform for creative professionals who want a clean, credible, and niche-friendly online presence without building an entire portfolio website from scratch.

Whether you're a **developer**, **designer**, **digital marketer**, or other creative professional — Hokori helps you showcase your work, credibility, and personality in one place.

The goal isn't to replace personal websites.

The goal is to give people **something meaningful that's far better than having nothing at all.**

---

## Why "Hokori"?

**Hokori (誇り)** is a Japanese word meaning **"Pride."**

Your work, projects, achievements, testimonials, case studies, and growth deserve a place you're proud to share.

## ✨ Version 1 Features

### 👤 Public Profile

Create a beautiful public profile with your own unique URL.

```txt
hokori.app/yourname
```

A profile designed around **professional identity**, not just social links.

---

### 📝 About Section

A short and focused bio to introduce yourself and what you do.

---

### 🛠 Skills Showcase

Highlight your skills and expertise in a clean, structured way.

Examples:

- React
- UI/UX Design
- SEO
- Paid Ads
- Backend Engineering
- Brand Strategy

---

### 🔗 Social Links

Add all your important platforms in one place.

Examples:

- GitHub
- LinkedIn
- X / Twitter
- Instagram
- Behance
- Dribbble
- Portfolio Website
- YouTube
- Personal Blog

---

### 📄 Resume / CV

Upload and showcase your professional resume directly on your profile.

---

### 💻 Developer Stats

For developers, Hokori will support:

- GitHub Stats
- GitHub Activity
- LeetCode Stats

Because your profile should actually reflect what you build.

---

### 🎨 Designer Integrations

For designers:

- Behance integration
- Dribbble showcase

Show your creative work without forcing visitors to jump between multiple platforms.

---

### 📆 Calendar Connections

Let people connect or schedule with you more easily.

Useful for:

- Freelancers
- Mentors
- Consultants
- Agency professionals

---

### 💬 Genuine Testimonials

A testimonial system designed to feel **credible**.

Instead of random copy-pasted praise, Hokori aims to support a **testimonial request flow**, making feedback more authentic and trustworthy.

---

### ✍️ Blogs, Articles & Case Studies

Publish:

- Blogs
- Articles
- Technical writeups
- Marketing case studies
- Design breakdowns
- Project learnings

Because sometimes your work deserves context, not just links.

---

### 🎨 Themes & Customization

Choose from beautiful themes and profile customization options to make your profile feel personal.

No generic template look.
